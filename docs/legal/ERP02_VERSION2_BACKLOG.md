# ERP-02 · Version 2.0 Backlog

Non-blocking enhancements identified during ERP-02 certification. **None of
these are required for V1 production release.** They are captured here so
they are not lost and can be prioritised for a future release.

## Priority conventions
- **P1** — high business value, no architectural change
- **P2** — meaningful UX or reporting improvement
- **P3** — strategic / long-horizon capabilities
